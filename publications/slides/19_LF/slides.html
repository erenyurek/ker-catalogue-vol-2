The context

  • We like to compare neural network activities (validation, calibration, benchmarking).
  • There are many ways to compare neuron-wise measures.
  • Statistics provides a wide range of two-sample tests.

The problem

  • Pair-wise measures are really interesting (correlation, causality, connectivity).
  • Measure values are not statistically indipendent.
  • Few statistical tests are applicable.

The idea

  • Eigenvectors point towards characteristic combinations of neurons.
  • Eigenvalues quantify the relevenace of the combinations.
  • Vectors can be compared with angles!
